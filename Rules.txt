100 Days of Code Challenge
---------------------------
0. Commit by tweeting that you are taking the challenge.
1. Code every day for 100 days for an hour.
2. You are allowed to miss one day of coding every two weeks.
2a.You are not allowed to miss two days in the row.
2b.Add the missed days at the end of the hundred days.
3. Watching videos / reading books or planning the project does not count in the one hour.
4. Tweet every day what you did that day.
